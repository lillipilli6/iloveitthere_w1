Our Mission Statement

Life isn’t quite back to how we knew it…

Victoria was dealt the short straw with Covid and the worst affected industry was that of good times! Help Melbourne get back to being Melbourne by supporting the backbone of good times. We were all hit hard during Covid but the nightowls of the city lost more than ⅓ of jobs, had hours dramatically reduced and were stood down where ‘working from home’ meant only mixing drinks for themselves. Hospitality was the most impacted sector in the country with Victoria being hit the hardest and fun free streets for over 7 months.

Now back on our feets and schooner in hand we want to see it stay that way, we want all of our old haunts to remain open and we want all of our favourite faces behind the bar still there and smiling.

This is only done through support. Whether that be a pint, a parma or a party - you’re support will ensure the survival of the unrivalled Melbourne bar scene.

Well - we also want to help by offering cool ass merch and displaying or pride on our chests. A significant amount goes to our “help for hospo’’ cause that helps struggling staff and also funds the creation of events that helps everyone have a good time and ensures Melbourne stays out ‘til late!
